Standing Rules of Order #

0.0 # Order of Business

  1. 0.0.1 # Adoption of the agenda
  2. 0.0.2 # Motions regarding minuting, including to record the meeting
  3. 0.0.3 # Approval of minutes
  4. 0.0.4 # Reports (see section 0.5.2)
  5. 0.0.5 # Business arising from reports
  6. 0.0.6 # Business postponed to this meeting or left over when a previous meeting in the same session adjourned
  7. 0.0.7 # Business on the agenda of the current meeting
  8. 0.0.8 # Other discussion items (see section 0.5)
  9. 0.0.9 # Other business brought without notice
  10. 0.0.10 # Adjournment
